Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to apply for Brgy ID?

    STEPS:

    1. Register or Log-in to QC eServices: https://qceservices.quezoncity.gov.ph/

    2. Click: QC ID eApplication.

    3. A step-by-step instruction will be shown for your guidance.

    4. A notification will be sent upon approval of your application. Should your application be rejected, applicant will be informed of the reason for rejection. Simply re-apply and submit corrected data/documents.

    REMINDERS:

    1. Strictly one QC eServices Account for one email address used.

    2. If applying for other family members, make sure to use a different email address and different QC eServices account.

    3. Disclose the correct and active contact numbers as you may receive notifications via your contact number.
